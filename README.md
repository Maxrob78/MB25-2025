# MB25-2025: Pack Opener & Football Manager

**MB25** is a card collection and financial management game developed in **Python** using the **Pygame** library. Open packs, manage your budget, invest in businesses, and build the ultimate team to compete against your rivals!

## ðŸŒŸ Key Features

* **Booster Pack System:** 4 distinct types of packs (**Basic, Icon, Ballon d'Or, Special**) with realistic drop rates and rarity logic.
* **Massive Collection:** Over **100 unique cards** with multiple rarity tiers, including **Common, Rare, Epic, Mythic, Legendary, TOTY, and ICON**.
* **Integrated Economy:** Earn money through matches or generate **passive income** by acquiring major businesses (e.g., *AirFrance, PSG*).
* **Luxury Store:** Use your earnings to buy **sports cars, aircraft, or official kits** to enhance your profile.
* **Match Mode:** Strategic **11-player squad building**. Challenge the AI with a score engine based on attack, defense, and rarity bonuses.
* **Training Mini-game:** A built-in **2D football simulator** featuring a custom physics engine, ball collisions, and advanced mechanics like **curve shots** and **roulettes**.
